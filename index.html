Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

WinSlice is built on a simple principle — your data is yours.

No Personal Data Collected

WinSlice does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account is required to use the app. We do not know who you are, where you are, or how you use the app.

Location

WinSlice requests access to your device location solely to detect your state and apply the correct local tax rate. Your location is used on-device only and is never transmitted to our servers, stored, or shared with any third party.

No Advertising

WinSlice contains no advertisements and no advertising SDKs. We do not use your data for advertising purposes and do not share any information with advertising networks.

No Third-Party Tracking

WinSlice does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry tools. We do not track how you use the app, how often you open it, or what features you use.

Lottery Data

WinSlice fetches publicly available lottery jackpot and results data from a third-party lottery data provider. This fetch is anonymous and contains no personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

WinSlice is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
